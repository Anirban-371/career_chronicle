About Us

"Take time to appreciate your employees and they will reciprocate in a thousand ways" -Bob Nelson

We are a mixed group of young,agile and experienced entrepreneur from various fields of IT,Core Engineering, Sales and Media, joined hands to help private organisations to meet their demand of quality manpower and individuals to get their dream jobs. We are here to facilitate bussiness to find employment and job seekers to get employed beyond their regional borders.

Career Chronicle is a Human Resource Organization working hard to create a long term relationship with the small, medium and large sized enterprises helping them to get eligible experienced and fresh workforce that directly reflects to the success of the organisation. For private clients, we understand their requirements. Gather the best avaliable minds. Put the resouces to test, bringing out the best among them and make them deployment ready. For individuals, we keep keen interest of learning their strengths and finding them a job of their choice.

Services

At Career Chronicle its much more than just Jobs, Employees and Employers.

 Permanent Jobs

We as career consultants, find resources for interested clients looking to hire on permanent basis.

 Contract Jobs

We provide manpower to organisations with demands to hire people on contract for limited period or projects.

 Internship

Starting from oraganisations to individuals, we help to find interns and provide internship respectively.

 Interviews

 Dream Jobs

We all have dreams and one part of it is taken by our 'dream company'.We help registered individuals with knowledge of openings in their prefered organisations and get through it.

 References

We act as a power booster for the job seeking individuals helping them to get references from authenticate personnel, from the organisation of their choice.
